## **1. Risk Management Basics**
**Risk** is the probability of loss due to a threat.
*   **Goal:** Reduce threats to an **acceptable level** (not zero) and maintain it.
*   **Process:** Frame the Risk $\rightarrow$ Assess the Risk $\rightarrow$ Respond to the Risk.

> [!important] **The Golden Rule**
> The cost of the countermeasure should never exceed the value of the asset being protected.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Qualitative** | **Opinions & Scenarios.** Subjective ranking. | **Risk Matrix** (Heat Map), Likelihood vs. Impact. |

### **Mitigation Strategies**
*   **Accept (Retain):** Acknowledge risk, take no immediate action.
*   **Reduce:** Implement controls (firewalls, patches).
*   **Avoid:** Stop the activity causing the risk.
*   **Transfer (Share):** Outsourcing or Insurance.
