## **1. Governance**
**IT Security Governance** determines decision-making authority for risks, ensures accountability, and aligns security with business goals/regulations.

### **Data Roles**
| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Data Owner** | Classifies data and ensures compliance. |
| **Data Controller** | Determines the *purpose* of data processing. |
| **Data Processor** | Processes data on behalf of the controller. |
| **Data Custodian** | Implements **technical controls** (security measures). |
| **Data Steward** | Aligns data with business needs & regulations. |
| **Data Protection Officer** | Oversees the overall protection strategy. |

### **Policies & HR Guidelines**

## **2. The Ethics of Cybersecurity**

### **Ethical Frameworks**
*   **âš–ï¸ Utilitarian:** Focus on **consequences** (Greatest good for the greatest number).
*   **ğŸ‘¤ Rights:** Focus on individual **choices/rights** (Privacy, Truth, Safety).
*   **ğŸ¤ Common Good:** Focus on benefits to the **community** and shared values.

### **Computer Ethics (10 Commandments)**
*   **DON'T:** Harm others, interfere with work, snoop, steal, bear false witness, pirate software, or use resources without permission.
*   **DO:** Think about social consequences and respect fellow humans.

### **Cybercrime Categories**
1.  **Targeted:** Computer is the victim (Hacking, Malware).
2.  **Assisted:** Computer is a tool (Fraud, Theft).
3.  **Incidental:** Computer holds evidence (Storing contraband).

## **4. IT Security Management Frameworks**

### **ISO/IEC 27000 Series**
A universal framework comprising **12 Domains** (Risk, Policy, Asset Mgmt, Access Control, Incident Mgmt, Compliance, etc.).

*   **Objectives vs. Controls:**
    *   *Control Objectives:* High-level requirements (Checklist).
    *   *Controls:* Guidelines on **how** to implement them.
*   **Statement of Applicability (SOA):** Tailors controls to the organization's **CIA Triad** priorities.
*   **Data States:** Controls address data **In Process**, **At Rest**, and **In Transit**.

### **Other Standards**

| Framework | Purpose | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NIST Workforce** | **Hiring/Skills** | Standardizes cybersecurity job roles (Analyze, Protect, Investigate, etc.). |
| **CIS Controls** | **Defense Strategy** | Prioritized controls based on resources: **Basic**, **Foundational**, **Organizational**. |
| **CSA (Cloud)** | **Cloud Security** | **CCM (Cloud Controls Matrix):** The de-facto standard for cloud assurance (197 controls). |

### **Audits & Certifications**
*   **SSAE 18 / SOC 2:** Audit reports for service providers.
    *   *Type I:* Snapshot in time.
    *   *Type II:* Managed over time (min. 6 months).
*   **CMMC:** Certification for **DoD Contractors**.
    *   5 Levels: From "Basic Cyber Hygiene" to "Advanced/APT Defense".
