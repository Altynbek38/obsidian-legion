## **3. Sharing Standards**
To automate the exchange of intelligence, specific open standards are used:

> [!abstract] **The Standards Trio**
> *   **STIX:** (Structured Threat Information Expression) The **File Format** / Specifications.
> *   **TAXII:** (Trusted Automated Exchange) The **Transport Protocol** (over HTTPS).
> *   **CybOX:** (Cyber Observable Expression) Standardized schema for describing network events/properties.
